SYNAPSE

About Us

Synapse is the Western dance club of RNSIT. Dancing to the tune of music, understanding the rhythm, and choreographing the moves are a few of the things that the synapse team is capable of. The members of Synapse are well versed with traditional as well new era dance forms and techniques

Introduction

Dance is the hidden language of the soul.Synapse represents the neural connections made through movement, where each dance move is a unique connection in the grand story of our college's cultural landscape. It's where the rhythms of life meet the art of expression, forging a vibrant and electrifying experience for both the dancers and the audience.

ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS

CARVAAN

IISC college, organized a western Dance competition,in which more the 20 teams participated, Where we proudly represented our collegeand qualified for the national round.

“HIP HOP INDIA CHAMPIONSHIP”

In the inter-college fest conducted by NITK “HIP HOP INDIA CHAMPIONSHIP” we represented our college RNSIT as well as karnataka in this event and became the top 6 qualifying team in this competition.

SJBIT competition

SJBIT college, organized a western Dance competition,in which more the 10 teams participated, Where we proudly represented our college and banged 2nd place in that inter college competition.
