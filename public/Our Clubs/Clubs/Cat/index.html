About Us

CAT at RNSIT is a dynamic and passionate group of students whose collective dedication and creativity breathe life into the rich tapestry of our college's cultural identity. Through a wide range of events, performances, and initiatives, they ensure that our college's culture continues to shine and evolve, fostering a sense of unity and pride among the entire student body.

As the cultural pillar of our college, the CAT endeavors to create a vibrant and inclusive environment where students from all backgrounds can express themselves creatively. Their collective efforts not only showcase the diverse talents of our student body but also bridge cultural gaps, fostering a sense of belonging and unity among our community. ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS

PANTHEON

Pantheon is a bi-annual inter college fest organized by RNS Institute of Technology and is the pride and joy of both the students as well as the Cultural Activity Team. The word Pantheon refers to the deities of a religion collectively and it is safe to say that it Lives up to its name. It is our vibrant and captivating cultural fest, a celebration of diversity and creativity! Our fest is an enchanting showcase of various traditions, art forms, and cultural expressions that come together to create an immersive experience for all attendees.

PARICHAY

Parichay is the intra-college fest hosted every alternate year at RNS Institute of Technology. Parichay pits all the branches of the college against each other in a fierce competition for two trophies namely, The Spirit of Parichay and the Talent of Parichay, It proudly hosts over several events organized by the Events committee of the Cultural Activity Team. The Spirit of Parichay is awarded to the branch with the most number of participants in all events and the Talent of Parichay is awarded to the branch that wins the most number of events.
