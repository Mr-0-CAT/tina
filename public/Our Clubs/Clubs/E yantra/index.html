Youth Red Cross Society

About Us

Youth Red Cross Society is task force in RNSIT with more than 150 Volunteers. It has undertaken many social programs. Youth Red Cross Society- YRCS is coordinated by Dr. Mallikarjun H M, Dept. of CSE- AI & ML.

Introduction

YRCS Staff Team consisting, Dr. Shobha H Prasad, Librarian, Prathap N, PED, Dr. Mallikarjun H M, Dept. of CSE-AI & ML, Dr. Harsha S, HoD, Dept. of CSE-AI & ML, Sachin Kulkarni, Dep.t of CV, Prof. Meenakshi, Dept. of CSE, Dr. Prakasha S, Dept. of ISE, Prof. Nirmala M S, , Dept. of ECE, Prof. Shwetha, , Dept. of EEE, Prof. Bharat Kumar, , Dept. of ME, Radha, , Dept. of CSE-DS, Dr. Manjunath N, Dept. of ME, Prof. Hemanth, Dept. of MCA, Dr. Deepak Kumar D, Dept. of MBA

ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS

YRCS, NSS Conducted Road Safety event

. Team conducted awareness campaigns related to the causes of road accidents and measures to prevent them. Event organised by replacing Barricades with plants by using cement pots (16 pcs).

Physical Education of RNSIT

In association with Lions club south Bengaluru organized “Blood Donation festival” on July 7, 2023. Team mobilized 324 units of blood in today’s Blood Donation festival. 4 blood banks visited campus namely Lions Club, Narayana Hrudayalaya, Indira Gandhi, Sanjaya Gandhi. Saplings are distributed to blood donors

Swachhata Andolan

Under this faculties and students visited Channasandra village, Anganawadi, labours staying in tent taught them wastage segregation dry and wet separately. Created awareness on more usage of cloth bags instead of plastic covers.

World Environment Day

. Dr. Ramesh Babu H S, Principal RNSIT inaugurated the event and motivated gatherings. 5 Faculties and more than 150 student volunteers joined the event. Team planted 8 rose Saplings in front of Canara Bank in RNSIT campus. Volunteers actively participated in the event by picking plastic covers at our campus (Swachhata drive). 300 seed balls were distributed to students by NSS-YRCS Staff Coordinators.
