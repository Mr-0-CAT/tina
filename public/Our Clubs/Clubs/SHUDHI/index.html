SHUDDI

About Us

Are you passionate about creating a cleaner and greener world? Do you want to make a positive impact on our environment? Join Shuddi, our waste management club, and be part of the change! Shuddi is all about promoting responsible waste management practices and raising awareness about environmental sustainability. Together, we can make a difference by reducing waste, recycling effectively, and educating our community.

As a member of Shuddi, you'll have the opportunity to participate in engaging activities such as waste clean-up drives, recycling campaigns, and educational workshops. You'll learn valuable skills, connect with like-minded individuals, and contribute to building a more sustainable future. Together, let's turn waste into opportunity and make a real difference.
