About Us

Amidst the monotonous life of college, there exist a hidden gem that thrives on creativity and the subtle art of story telling. "ACTKRIT" club, the pioneer of the college's cultural landscape, captures the essence of art in its rawest form.

Introduction

The club offers a vibrant array of artistic expressions, encompassing various forms of creative storytelling. This includes the dynamic world of street plays, where actors engage with audiences in public spaces to convey powerful messages. Skits bring humor and thought-provoking narratives to life through concise yet impactful performances. Monologues offer a platform for individual actors to delve deep into emotions and characters, providing intimate moments of introspection. Join us to unlock your creativity and explore these diverse avenues of artistic expression!

ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS

HALLA BOL

Carv-Hindi, which is the drama club of RVCE, organised a Streetplay competition, in which more than 15 teams participated , where we proudly represented our college. We presented on the theme of humanity vs cruelty in the society and how in the end humanity always conquers all the evil in the society.

Incident'23 by NITK

In the inter-college fest conducted by NITK, Incident '23 , we participated in Streetplay competition where various colleges from different states participated. Here we bagged the second prize.

Anti -ragging campaign

Not only we participate in inter-college events, we also spread awareness through our art in the campus. One such instance was a Streetplay on Anti-Ragging which we did in our college, to educate the students about the dangerous effect of ragging in other students lives.
