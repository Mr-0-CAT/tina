About Us

AMURA stands for "Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Undergraduate Research Association." It is the Student Club of the Department of AI & ML at RNSIT (RNS Institute of Technology). The club's primary focus is to foster interest, knowledge, and research in the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning among undergraduate students. AMURA may offer various activities such as seminars, workshops, projects, and discussions to help students explore and delve deeper into the world of AI and ML.

Introduction

To create a competitive learning environment and enliven campus life at the Department of AI & ML, RNSIT, host hackathons, research symposiums, workshops, and collaborations with local tech companies while promoting social activities and mentorship programs.
