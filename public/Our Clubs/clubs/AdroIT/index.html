AdroIT

About Us

AdroIT Club is a unique student organization that immerses members in emerging tech domains like data analytics, cybersecurity, and blockchain. It prioritizes hands-on learning through real-world projects and collaboration with peers. By offering practical experiences and industry-relevant projects, AdroIT equips students with essential skills for success in the tech-driven world.

Introduction

Adroit is a student-friendly club of the CSE Department, established by Sharanya N, Shriya Desai, Sunayana J B on October 22nd 2022 under the guidance and support of Dr. Kiran P (HOD, CSE) and Dr. Sampada K S. The club has successfully organized numerous events, including engaging pep talks with industry experts, offering dedicated courses for various domains, hosting informative workshops, conducting exciting hackathons, and arranging sessions to showcase your projects.
