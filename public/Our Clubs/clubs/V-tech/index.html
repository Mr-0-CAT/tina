About Us

Our club serves as a catalyst for aspiring ECE students, fostering their interest in VLSI and embedded systems. Through engaging activities such as technical talks, workshops, and courses, we provide valuable insights into current trends and opportunities within the core domain.

Introduction

Our alumni meets create a network of support and inspiration, while technical tests challenge and enhance the skills of our members. Join us in this journey of exploration and empowerment in the dynamic field of VLSI and embedded systems.
