Enlightera

About Us

Enlightera serves as a vibrant community where students come together to discuss, analyze, and share their passion for literature, encouraging intellectual growth and fostering a lifelong love for the written word. Through various activities, book discussions, and creative writing sessions, the club strives to create a space where members can not only find solace in the beauty of words but also expand their horizons through the rich tapestry of literary works. With a commitment to nurturing both literary enthusiasts and budding writers, Enlightera stands as a beacon of literary enlightenment within our college.

Introduction

Enlightera, founded on June 15, 2022, in our college, is a literature club dedicated to fostering a deep appreciation for the joy of literature and imparting the significance of literary exploration to its members.
