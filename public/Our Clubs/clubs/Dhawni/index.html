DHWANI

About Us

Dhwani, the music band of RNSIT, comprises of talented musicians who have indulged at various aspects of the musical world. Being the voice of RNS, Dhwani have performed in various events inside and outside college. They have bewitched crowds everywhere with their versatile Indian and Indo-western style of music!

In 2021-22, Dhwani band experienced remarkable growth. They kicked off with a performance at the prestigious Taj during an HR meet and secured first place in The Gig by the National School of Journalism. Further victories included winning Desha Bhakti Geethe in Mysore, a beatboxing competition, and the Battle of Bands by the Global Academy of Technology. Their talents were showcased at the intra-college fest PARICHAY, fostering band cohesion. Lastly, Dhwani proudly represented RNSIT at VTU's Prathibhotsava, participating in various music competitions and gaining exposure to talent from over 100 colleges.
