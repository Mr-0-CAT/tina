About Us

The AIKYA Student Club is a student body of the CSE department, RNSIT which was established in the year 2011.The club organized two days of various events for the students of the department in March 2023.The first day had a technical event and the second day had non- technical events. Both of these had over 120 participants competing to win the cash prize. The events served as a refreshing break in the mid of a long semester for everyone.

We held events for students of the CSE(and allied branches),ISE and AIML branches. Over 170 students participated in the events hosted over the course of two days July 7th and 8th 2023.

*Aikya* 2023

Multiverse of Madness

A mystery adventure game, which was an elaborate escapade into different “universes” or themed rooms such as Sherlock Holmes, Cricket, Football, Anime etc

Call Of Duty Mobile

A battle royale of all students playing the game was held. All the participants had to battle against each other in a virtual battleground until there was only one final survivor, crowned as the winner.

Cooking without fire

It was an amazing display of dishes created by the students without using any kind of heat. All participants in teams of two, brought their own requirements an completed the dishes in the stipulated time.
