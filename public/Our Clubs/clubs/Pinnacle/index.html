Pinnacle

About Us

"Pinnacle" is an annual extravaganza organized by the Information Science and Engineering (ISE) department of RNSIT. This vibrant fest features a wide array of exciting events, including dancing, singing, treasure hunts, and much more. It serves as a platform for students to showcase their talents and creativity while fostering a sense of unity and community within the department. Pinnacle's electrifying atmosphere and diverse activities make it a must-attend event for all RNSIT students. Get ready to experience the pinnacle of fun and excitement!

Introduction

Pinnacle College Club is a vibrant organization dedicated to nurturing talent and creativity among our college students. We offer a diverse range of events and activities that enrich the college experience.Beyond our core events, Pinnacle offers a wide array of enjoyable activities, both indoors and outdoors, such as singing, dancing, and thrilling treasure hunts. These activities ensure a well-rounded and unforgettable college journey. Join us to explore your passions and create lasting memories while growing personally and academically.

ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS

Shark Tank

Our marquee event, "Shark Tank," challenges aspiring entrepreneurs to pitch their innovative business ideas, compete for funding, and receive valuable mentorship from seasoned professionals.

Debates

Pinnacle hosts stimulating debates on a variety of topics, promoting critical thinking and effective communication skills among participants. It's an ideal platform to sharpen your argumentative prowess.

Coding Competitions

For tech enthusiasts, our coding competitions provide an opportunity to showcase coding skills, problem-solving abilities, and collaboration with fellow students.
