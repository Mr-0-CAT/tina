INDIAN INSTITUE OF ELECTRONICS AND TELECOMMUNICATION ENGINEERS

About Us

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is India's premier professional society dedicated to the relentless advancement of science and technology in the realm of electronics and telecommunications. Established with a steadfast commitment to fostering innovation and progress, the IETE plays a pivotal role in shaping the technological landscape of the nation.

Introduction

Department of Electronics and communication, have an active IETE student branch. The IETE ISF give financial and technical support to conduct many technical activities like national conference, workshop, technical talk etc.
