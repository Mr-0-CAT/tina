About Us

As the only club representing science in RNSIT,our mission is to make science fun, engaging, and accessible to everyone. So, whether you're a budding scientist or just someone who's curious about the world around them, this is the place for you!

Join us in this incredible journey of curiosity and exploration, where you'll laugh, learn, and make memories that will last a lifetime. Our teachers are here to guide you through the world of science with enthusiasm, encouragement, and a good dose of humor.

ACTHIVMENTS

The Sci Pi Club's crowning achievement is undoubtedly the spectacular Sci-Fi Fest that took place on the 1st of August. The fest featured captivating activities like collage making, model making, a futuristic 'Shark Tank' , an intellectual quiz, and the highly anticipated treasure hunt!Among these captivating events, the treasure hunt stood out as an interstellar hit! It garnered an overwhelming response from our fellow explorers, with more than 80 teams joining the game. We can't wait to continue our journey of exploration, learning, and fun with all of you. Stay tuned for more stellar adventures to come!So, what are you waiting for? Come and be a part of our Science Club – where science is not just a subject, it's an adventure!
