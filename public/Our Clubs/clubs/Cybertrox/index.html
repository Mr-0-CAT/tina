Cybotryx

About Us

Welcome to a dynamic hub that ignites the passion of tech enthusiasts! At our core, we are explorers, delving into the captivating realms of robotics and augmented reality. Through engaging, hands-on projects, we cultivate creativity, innovation, and teamwork among our members. Join us and experience the exhilaration of constructing robots from the ground up, and dive headfirst into the immersive world of augmented reality.

Whether you're a seasoned tech wizard or just beginning your journey, our community is the perfect place to nurture your tech talents and embark on exciting adventures in the ever-evolving worlds of robotics and augmented reality.

ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS

Obstacle Avoidance Robot

A car that detects obstacles through Ultrasonic Sensors and IR Sensors controlled by an Arduino to navigate through a path.

Humanoid Robot "Shrek"

An advanced humanoid robot capable of motion, stabilization, and activities. Uses multiple servo motors and a Raspberry Pi for movement and control.
