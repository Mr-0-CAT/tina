Insights

VTU FEST

"Yuvotsava 2023" , VTU fest was conducted in Nagarjuna college of engineering and technology. we performed one of the folk dances of Karnataka - Jade kolata.

HR meet

The HR meet was held in Taj Vivantha. We incorporated elements of Bharatanatyam, Kathak, and Yakshagana into our fusion dance performance.

NEXUS 2023

Nexus is a fest conducted by Jain University. We the classical team of rnsit, the Natarang presented "Draupadi vastraparana"-a small part of Draupadi 's life. Her valour, and through her that of all the empowered women

Reach Out to us

Are you passionate about dance? Join NATARANG, RNSIT's classical dance club. Dance is the art of expressing emotion through movement. India boasts a rich history of diverse dance styles. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or a beginner, NATARANG welcomes all skill levels. Join us to celebrate and explore the beauty of Indian dance forms. Let's create captivating performances together and share the joy of dance with our audience. Join NATARANG and let dance be your expressive outlet.
