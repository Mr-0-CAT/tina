Product Development Club

About Us

The vision of the product development club is to develop a culture of design thinking and user-centric product development in the institute through Design Challenges, Impactful Projects, Workshops, and more.

At the PDC, we are excited by technology and the liberating impact that it can have. We are equally driven by problems that matter, Our projects always strive to solve high-impact problems and meet unmet needs. We ideate, iterate, and transform ideas into cutting-edge solutions that redefine the world of possibilities.

ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS

RNSIT Campus Buggy

In 6th semester we have developed an battery operated 5-seater vehicle which was branded as “RNSIT Campus Buggy”. The main aim of the vehicle was to provide ease of travel and comfort to travel around the campus.

Automated Wheel Chair

At PDC we have developed a Automated Wheel Chair whose primary goal is to empower those who are physically challenged. The wheelchair that we have designed eliminates all the limitations faced by the convention wheelchair. The wheelchair is a completely electric and can be controlled autonomously by a mobile phone.
