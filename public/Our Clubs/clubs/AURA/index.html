About Us

AURA, the embodiment of confidence at RNS, stands as a shining beacon of dedication and talent within the college community. With unwavering determination, they continuously hone their skills, setting the bar high for excellence. Their impressive track record in inter-college competitions, where they have clinched numerous prizes, speaks volumes about their prowess in the world of fashion.

AURA's inclusivity is a testament to their commitment to diversity, drawing students from various academic backgrounds, including engineering, MBA, MCA, and architecture, all united under the banner of RNS. The team's meticulous training regimen and extensive preparation ahead of college fests ensure that their performances are nothing short of flawless. Whether on home turf or in distant college venues, AURA leaves audiences spellbound with their breathtaking shows, making them a true source of pride for RNS.

ACTHIVMENTS

Aura Club is no stranger to the world of inter-college competitions, having proudly participated in more than 13 prestigious events. Their consistent involvement in such contests highlights their commitment to pushing the boundaries of excellence in fashion and performance. With each competition, they bring their A-game, showcasing their refined skills and confidence to the fullest. AURA's track record of active engagement in these events is a testament to their dedication and their enduring desire to excel on a broader stage. They continue to shine as a beacon of talent and achievement within the college community.
