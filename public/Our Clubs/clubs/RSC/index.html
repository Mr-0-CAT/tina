About Us

RSC (RNSIT Sports Club) is a student club of RNSIT that comprises of sports enthusiasts who motivate each other to stride towards achieving excellence in their respective sports. RSC actively conducts various sporting events and activities through out the year. RSC was established to ensure that everyone in college was given a platform to showcase their sporting talents and passion along with conducting various events.

At RSC, we believe that sports play a vital role in shaping a well-rounded college experience. Our mission is to provide a platform for every student to showcase their sporting talents and nurture their passion. Beyond that, we are committed to creating a vibrant sports culture within the college community. Throughout the year, RSC organizes a diverse range of sporting events and activities, encouraging participation from all corners of the campus. We're not just a club; we're a community that thrives on the shared love for sports, teamwork, and the pursuit of excellence. Join RSC and be part of a journey where passion meets potential, and where you can be a true sports enthusiast!

Activities & Events

Agones
Agones is the intra-college sports fest. Various sporting events are organised across 3 days. The winners and runners of each event are awarded stipulated points which influences the overall standing of their department. At the end, a rolling trophy is presented to the department with highest points.
StepUp
StepUp is the annual 5K run organised on a grand scale by RSC. The run takes place around the RNSIT campus surroundings. StepUp is organised to cater for noble causes like adoption of a leopard, feed the poor or planting a sapling for each registation.
RPL
RNS Premiere League was a cricket tournament organised by RSC in which 6 teams competed including an alumni team. The tournament was a spectacle to the viewers while being organised very professionally. Selection trials were conducted to shortlist from the huge array of players. The selected players were then auctioned to the teams mimicing the Indian Premiere league (IPL).
